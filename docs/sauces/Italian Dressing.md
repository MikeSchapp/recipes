# Italian Dressing

- 3/4 cup olive oil
- 1/4 Cup Red or White Wine Vinegar
- 1 tsp Garlic Powder
- 1 tsp Dried Oregano
- 1 tsp Dried Basil
- 1/2 tsp Onion Powder
- 1/2 tsp Crushed Red Pepper
- 3/4 tsp Salt
- 1/2 tsp Pepper
- 1 tsp Lemon Juice

## Instructions

1. Mix all together!
2. Shake before using
